How would you define an engaged vs. unengaged user¶

An engaged

According to accessdevelopment bernardmarr.com

User engagement is a measurement of how much your customer interacts with a product or service you offer. Its a measurement of user behavior (frequency or depth of interaction) over time.

So the taking into account available data one of the most obvious metric is to check if client is actively uses product - if is making a transactions with card.

Another metrics could be, if client is responding to company notification.

We could measure as well if client is promoting a company by number of referrals or number of mention (ex. hashtags with compane name) in social media.

Given the available data I'll narrow the problem to measurement, whether client actively using the product.

user engagement measure

  • engaged client - in the last X days client have mage Y transacion,
  • unengaged client - in the last X days client have not made Y transacion,
